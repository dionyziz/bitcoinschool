Stipends

We have two sources of funding for student travel. The first is from the National Science Foundation (NSF) and will be able to fund 10 student travel grants of up to 1000 USD each for students attending a US-based university, and the second is from IOHK and will be able to fund 6 students based in Greece or Ukraine.

Email us by March 31st and include your CV and a short statement of why you are interested in attending the school. Also have your academic advisor directly email us with a short letter of support.

Submissions

We are soliciting ideas for presentations of roughly 10-20 minutes each. To submit an idea for a talk, please send by April 15 a short abstract to organizers@bitcoinschool.gr and specify how much time you'd like.
