About

We're creating the first summer school on blockchain technologies to bridge the gap between the cryptocurrency community and academia. We welcome students, researchers and professionals with an interest in cryptocurrencies and blockchains to join us.

Location

Registrations will open in March 2016.

The school will be hosted at the Corfu Imperial Hotel.
